Hurdles We Faced:
Less liquidity for OTC cryptocurrency trading options.
Security protocol integration for a crypto payment gateway is more challenging.
Implementing secured crypto ATMs in specified regions was not so easy.
Legal Guidance from experts for launching a crypto payment processor.   


Our Team’s Research Findings:
Our client was clear about providing a secure platform for crypto transactions, and bulk trades at one stop. We sincerely noted their intention and integrated the KYB (Know your Business) feature for merchants to enhance the trustworthiness of the payment gateway.
As OTC trading focuses only on a limited set of investors, we were requested to implement numerous features. So, our developer team made a crypto payment gateway platform with a myriad of modules that help for faster transactions, instant conversion rates, and potential OTC trading.
We also analyzed to provide a unique feature that many platforms failed to offer for its users. There our technical team came up with the idea of implementing crypto ATM where cryptos can be converted to cash and taken out from ATMs.
